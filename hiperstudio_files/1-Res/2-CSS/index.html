San Diego, California

Bordered by the Pacific Ocean to the west, the Anza-Borrego Desert and the Laguna Mountains to the east, and Mexico to the south, the diverse neighborhoods of San Diego are spread out over 4,200 square miles, offering endless opportunities for exploration and activities.

SeaWorld San Diego

SeaWorld San Diego is an animal theme park, oceanarium, outside aquarium, and marine mammal park, located in San Diego, California, United States. The park is owned by SeaWorld Entertainment. Here are some of the attractions:

  • Killer Whales: Up Close
  • One Ocean - A Shamu Show
  • Dolphin Days - Meet the Whale and Dolphin Family

Beaches in San Diego

With 70 miles of pristine coastline, year-around sunshine and mild temperatures, you can enjoy the surf and sand almost any day of the year. For visitors and locals alike, the beach is a veritable wonderland and the place to spend entire days with family and friends. Here are some of the best sun-kissed beaches:

  1. La Jolla Shores Beach
  2. Torrey Pines State Beach and Park
  3. Mission Beach
  4. Coronado Municipal Beach
